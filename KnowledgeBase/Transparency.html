Transparency


Information Collection And Use


Workvivo is committed to complete transparency on what information is collected and how it is used and this is detailed to users of the system in the Workvivo privacy policy. The basic data we hold is a very short list of information, also detailed below and is used to set up an employee on our system and to help improve our Service to customers.


Types of Data Collected

Personal Data
- First name
- Last name
- Email address
- Date of birth
- Hire date
- Job title
- Team
- Location
- Contact phone number
- Profile photo
- Cookies and Usage Data



Usage Data

We may collect information that a browser sends whenever a user visits our Service or when users access the Service by or through a mobile device ('Usage Data'). This Usage Data may include information such as the computer's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address), browser type browser version, the pages of our Service that a user visits, the time and date of the users visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers and other diagnostic data. When a user accesses the Service by or through a mobile device, this Usage Data may include information such as the type of mobile device used, mobile device unique ID the IP address of the users mobile device, mobile operating system the type of mobile Internet browser used, unique device identifiers and other diagnostic data.



Tracking Cookies Data

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a website and stored on a users device. Tracking technologies also used are beacons & tags & and scripts to collect and track information and to improve and analyse our Service. A user can instruct their browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if a user does not accept cookies they may not be able to use some portions of our Service.

Examples of Cookies we use:
- Session Cookies: We use Session Cookies to operate our Service.
- Preference Cookies: We use Preference Cookies to remember a users preferences and various settings.

- Security Cookies: We use Security Cookies for security purposes.



Use of Data

The legal basis for the processing of the above information is consent. Users indicate their agreement with the Workvivo privacy policy on first login.
Workvivo Limited uses the collected data for various purposes:

- To provide and maintain our Service
- To notify about changes to our Service
- To allow users to participate in interactive features of our Service when they choose to do so - To provide customer support
- To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our Service
- To monitor the usage of our Service
- To detect, prevent and address technical issues
- Allows automatic generation of messages such as welcome to new hires and also recognition of work anniversaries based on hire date information.
- Provide an ability to report and provide trends on employee feedback based on tenure, team, age profile, location.

Statistical Data Use - Workvivo does not claim ownership of the Customer Data or personally identifiable data provided during the operation of the Service. Workvivo may however utilize anonymous usage statistics and performance metrics to improve and administer the Service for Workvivo’s internal use and other lawful purposes. Such data may include without limitation the number of records held in the Service the number and types of transactions configurations reports processed in the Service and the performance results for the Service . Nothing herein shall be interpreted as prohibiting Workvivo from utilising the aggregated statistics for the purposes of operating Workvivo’s business, provided that Workvivo’s use of aggregated statistics will not reveal the identity whether directly or indirectly of Customer any individual or any specific data entered by Customer or any individual into the Service.



Retention of Data

Workvivo Limited will retain a user’s Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in our Privacy Policy. Our policy is that the information held for each user is only held while the user is an active user of the system. When a user leaves the organisation that is a customer of Workvivo the user is removed from the Workvivo system and all personal data is immediately deleted. Aggregate data not identifiable to a user is held after a user has been deleted for the purposes of protecting the integrity of reporting systems. Where it is necessary to retain Personal Data to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations for example if we are required to retain your data to comply with applicable laws) resolve disputes and enforce our legal agreements and policies then we will do so. Workvivo Limited will also retain Usage Data for internal analysis purposes. Usage Data is generally retained for a shorter period of time except when this data is used to strengthen the security or to improve the functionality of our Service or we are legally obligated to retain this data for longer time periods.



Transfer of Data

We transfer all data, including Personal Data, to Ireland and process it there. The Workvivo privacy policy provides transparency on this to all users including users located outside of Ireland and EU where possible data transfer including Personal Data, may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of a users state province country or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ than those from the users jurisdiction. A users consent to the Privacy Policy represents the users agreement to that transfer. Workvivo Limited will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that a user data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy and no transfer of any Personal Data will take place to an organization or a country unless there are adequate controls in place including the security of the users data and other personal information.



Disclosure of Data

Legal Requirements

Security

The security of your data is important to us, and we undertake to keep the Personal Data confidential and take all appropriate technical and organisational measures to ensure a level of security for the Personal Data which is appropriate to the risks to individuals that may result from the accidental or unlawful destruction loss alteration unauthorised disclosure of, or access to the Personal Data.

Workvivo is a cloud-based solution, hosted in Amazon Web Services (AWS) in the eu-west-1 (Ireland) region. We leverage many AWS products and services to provide us with an infrastructure that is not only highly scalable, but also provides for maximum security Workvivo is designed for scalability fault tolerance and high security. Our system architecture minimises the external entry points to our infrastructure and highly leverages fine-grained permissions within the AWS ecosystem to ensure that the various services only have access to those resources they need.

Accountability

Workvivo Limited is registered with the Data Protection Commissioner (DPC) as a data processor Workvivo Limited aims to take reasonable steps to allow users to correct or amend or delete or limit the use of a user’s Personal Data. If a user wishes to be informed what Personal Data we hold about the user they can contact support@workvivo.com to request this.

In certain circumstances, user has the right:
- To access and receive a copy of the Personal Data we hold about them
- To rectify any Personal Data held about the user that is inaccurate
- To request the deletion of Personal Data held about the user A user has the right to data portability for the information you provide to Workvivo Limited and Workvivo facilitates this. A user can request to obtain a copy of Personal Data in a commonly used electronic format so that you can manage and move it.

Please note that we may ask you to verify your identity before responding to such requests. We do not use any of your personal information for direct marketing.
